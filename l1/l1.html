How to

  1. Click everywhere on the dark box when you are ready;
  2. Wait the green;
  3. Click when the color changes (when you do a correct attempt, a dark dot turn green at the bottom);
  4. Repeat this 5 times (you have to collect 5 green dots);
  5. You can use the reset button to reset the attempt and start the test again;
  6. At the end, an average time and each attempts timings appear on the screen.
