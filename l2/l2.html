How does it work

The Dynamic Test is built in order to measure the reaction time related to the visual-motor coordination.
You just have to click (as quicker as you can) on the shapes that will appear on the bottom of the page.

  1. Click on "START" to begin;
  2. The test has no end so you must click on "STOP" to end it up.
  3. The "score" is simply calculated as a sum of the previus "Your time" values.
