About

Reaction time, also known as response time, is the interval between perceiving a stimulus and initiating a response to it. The main factors influencing time are:

  • Perception: the time your sense take to perceive the external stimulus;

  • Processing: the time your brain need to understand the stimulus;
  • Response: the time your body need to generate a response to the brain request.

This simple tool measures your reaction time. There are 2 different kinds of test:

Static Test

The goal of "static test" is to reduce the body response delay. You just have to click a box without moving the mouse. You should use this test to understand how fast can you respond to a stimulus.

Dynamic (aim) Test

The "dynamic test" esteem also your visual-motor coordination. You have to click on the shapes that appear in different positions on the page. This is the classic aim test/trainer used by video-game player to improve their aiming skills. Something like this.

You should use this test to improve your coordination and the reaction time to moving items. I do not recommend this test on mobile devices!
