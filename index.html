About

Reaction time, also known as response time, is the interval between perceiving a stimulus and initiating a response to it. The main factors influencing time are:

  • Perception: the time your sense take to perceive the external stimulus;

  • Processing: the time your brain need to understand the stimulus;
  • Response: the time your body need to generate a response to the brain request.

This simple tool measures your reaction time. There are 2 different kind of tests:

Static Test

The goal of static test is to reduce the body response delay. You just have to click a box without moving the mouse.

Dynamic (aim) Test

The dynamic test measures also your visual-motor coordination. You have to click the shapes which appears in different positions on the page. This is the classic aim test/trainer used by videoplayer to improve their aim skills. Such like this.
